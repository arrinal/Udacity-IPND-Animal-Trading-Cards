Wildcat

The wildcat is a cat that is easily found on the streets, even anywhere.

  • Scientific Name: Felis silvestris
  • Average Length: 43 - 91 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 21 years
  • Habitat: Savanna, Open Forest, Steppe

Wildcat have varied species which is like Pallas Cat, Leopard Cat, Jaguarundi, etc. Compared to other cats, he is a cat that has strong endurance which is able to survive in a slum environment.
